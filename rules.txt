## THE EQUIPMENT
Ludo consists of a circle board with four different colored bases in each corner. The first colored space
outside of each base is the start position. A path leads clockwise around the board returning to a path with
the same color as the base, then to the home column, which leads to the center home column. There are
four different colored sets of playing pieces that start off in their matching bases. Each set consists of 4
playing pieces. The dice is used for to include pawn into the game or to govern the movement of the
pawns.
